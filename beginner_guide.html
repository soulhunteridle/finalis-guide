The Formula

Everything in this game multiplies. Not adds. Here's the formula that covers all damage stats:

Final Damage = [(1 + a) × (1 + b) × (1 + c) × ...] − 1

Convert each stat from % to decimal first. So 500% becomes 5, and 1,625% becomes 16.25.

⚠ Convertion Rules: To convert percentage to multiplier, just do the percentage/100
Simple Example — Final Fire Damage (2 stats)
  • Final Fire Fire Damage: 20,898.77%
  • Fire Damage Increase: 1,117.32% → 11.1732
  • Flame Damage Amplification: 1,625% → 16.25
(1 + 11.1732) × (1 + 16.25) − 1
= 12.1732 × 17.25 − 1
= 208.9877 = 20,898.77% ✓

Stat names don't matter. "Fire Increase" and "Flame Amplification" are both just (1+x) in the formula.

Full Example — Hero Skill Damage (6 stats)
  • Final Hero Skill Damage: 3,214,070,698.4%
  • Hero Skill Damage Increase: 15,237% → 152.37
  • Hero Skill Damage Amplification: 500% → 5
  • Rune Hero Skill Damage Increase: 500% → 5
  • Rune Hero Skill Damage Amplification: 2,000% → 20
  • Increased Hero Skill Damage: 1,700% → 17
  • Symbol Hero Skill Damage Increase: 1,440% → 14.40
153.37 × 6 × 6 × 21 × 18 × 15.40 − 1
= 153.37 × 6
= 920.22 × 6
= 5,521.32 × 21
= 115,947.72× 18
= 2,087,058.96× 15.40
= 32,140,707.98 − 1
= 3,214,070,698.4% ✓
Key Rule: Always invest in your lowest stats first. Going from ×1 to ×2 doubles your damage. Going from ×100 to ×101 barely moves it. Fill the small ones first.
TLDR
  1. All damage stats multiply each other — not add
  2. Stat category names don't matter — they're all just (1+x)
  3. Always upgrade your smallest multiplier first for the biggest gain

Beginner Goals
Step 1 — Equipment to Mythic 1

Get these three equipment pieces to Mythic 1 first:

  • Scythe
  • Orb
  • Deco
Step 2 — Pull These 4 Mythic Passives

These are your staple passives regardless of build. Pull all 4 before deciding anything else.

  1. Power of Flames — Fire Amp
  2. Thunder's Fury — Thunder Amp
  3. Shadow Corrosion — Dark Amp
  4. Elemental Fusion — All Ele Amp

There's a 5th mythic passive (ignore def) in the same pool. It's not worth using — just ignore it if you pull it.

Step 3 — Consider Ancient 4 Scythe

Before spending diamonds on hero or skill pickup summons, consider pushing your Scythe to Ancient 4 first. Good boost-to-cost ratio at this stage.

Step 4 — Pick Your Build Identity

Your build = 1 element + 1 job. Pick one of each and focus everything there. For example like below:

⚔ Fire - Reaper
Smash / Crit Damage
Slot 1: Smash Expert
Slot 2: Elemental Fusion
Slot 3: Fire amp
🔮 Thunder - Archmage
Skill Damage
Slot 1: Magic Power Surge
Slot 2: Elemental Fusion
Slot 3: Thunder amp
💀 Dark - Dark Lord
Hero Skill Damage
Slot 1: Battle Command
Slot 2: Elemental Fusion
Slot 3: Dark amp

Smash Expert, Magic Power Surge, and Battle Command are lower rarity — you'll get them naturally while pulling for the staple mythics.

Which Build Should You Pick?

Personally I would suggest not to pick your build but let the game give you the build. How? By Following the available banner. There are 4 banners rotating every 2 weeks: New Skill, Existing Skill, New Hero, Existing Hero. Read all 4 together — they usually point toward one strong build combination.

Why the banner? Skills and heroes need 6★ or 9★ to be worth building around. The banner dramatically increases your pull rate for specific units — it's the only realistic way to hit those stars as a low spender. You need 18 dupes of mythic/hero skill to get to 6★ and 50 dupes of mythic/hero skill to get to 9★. Prepare at least 7 million diamond for skill and 17.5 million diamond for hero, if you wanna get them to 9★ so you don't have this unwanted condition where you stuck on them with 7 or 8 star that sometimes makes them practically useless or not optimized.

For your first 2 weeks: just follow the banner, no overthinking. After that you'll have enough context to decide for yourself whether the next banner is worth committing to.

TLDR
  1. Equipment to Mythic 1 first (Scythe, Orb, Deco)
  2. Pull the 4 staple mythic passives — ignore the 5th
  3. Consider Ancient 4 Scythe before pickup summons
  4. Check all 4 banners — build toward what they support
  5. First 2 weeks: follow the banner. After that: your call
  6. Slot 1 = job passive, Slot 2 = Elemental Fusion, Slot 3 = your element amp

Event Currency — What to Buy

There are always 2 events running with 2 different currencies. "What's the best buy?" — honestly, it depends. Here are your 3 options:

Option A — Equipment Summon Tickets

Great early value. Scales well and helps you hit Mythic 1 on equipment faster. Also lets you save your Diamonds for pickup summons later.

Option B — Costume Tickets

Back to the formula — costume attack power is only earned through costumes. If you have zero costumes you're missing entire multiplier categories. Here are all 5 costume categories:

Regular Costume Atk — any costume
Wing Costume Atk — wing costumes only
Ancient Costume Atk — ancient pieces only
Ancient Wing Costume Atk — ancient wing only
Costume Catalogue / Set Bonus — complete a set

Recommended priority to cover all 5 categories:

  1. 1-2× Mythic Wing Set (1 set consist of 1 wing + 3 others costumes, ~250 tickets) — covers Regular, Wing, and Catalogue in one set
  2. 1× Ancient Scythe — best boost-to-cost ratio for Ancient category
  3. 1× Ancient Wing — fills Ancient Wing category

Stop here. All 5 categories are now filled. Further costume purchases only stack into existing multipliers — diminishing returns.

Ancient set exists but shares catalogue with regular set — no new multiplier unlocked, not worth it.

⚠ Going beyond this is only for chasing titles, which costs a lot. Not recommended unless you really want to.
Option C — Treasure Dungeon Keys

Hoard these from the start. In late game they raise your job level — milestones at level 40 and 60 give huge boosts. Just accumulate, you will be able to use it once you unlock treasure dungeon and

Option D — Job Advancement key (1st or 2nd)

Can also be hoarded from the start. In late game they raise your job level — milestones at level 40 and 60 give huge boosts for job lv 1. Once you reach job level 60 you can consider switching to 2nd Job Advancement Key to get the initial easy level, then adjust back again switching between 1st and 2nd as you please.

Option E — Spirit Dungeno Key

Can also be hoarded from the start. Spirit is a new feature added to the game and they CAN'T be summoned using diamond, they can only be summoned using their own currency obtained from their specific dungeon, that's why hoarding this also needed as F2P or low spender.

TLDR
  1. Option A: Equipment tickets = best early value, saves diamonds for pickup
  2. Option B: Costume priority — Mythic Wing Set → Ancient Scythe → Ancient Wing → stop
  3. Option C: Treasure Dungeon keys — just hoard them, pay off big at level 40 and 60 job level
  4. Option D: 1st and 2nd Job Advancement Key — Can focus on 2nd Job after reaching level 60 job and then balancing again between 1st and 2nd in the future.
  5. Option E: Spirit Dungeon keys — Can be hoarded to help summoning spirits up to level 8-10 after unlocking it in the late game
  6. The choice is yours based on where you are in progression

Ability Rolls
Always Roll Option Atk Power

Equipment, attribute enhancement, soul enhancement — everything with an ability slot. Always pick Option Atk Power.

Why? Option Atk Power can only be earned through this system. Every other stat has multiple sources and is already stacked up. Option Atk Power starts at zero — going from (1+0) to (1+x) is the biggest possible jump per the formula.
Priority Order

Get everything to Special first, then you can also push to Legend if you want. Do NOT push to Mythic. Step 4-6 below is now OPTIONAL. The reason why pushing to legend is now optional will be written below after the sentence section.

Sentence Abilities (Unlocks at Stage 45-1)

Once you unlock Sentence, this becomes your dice priority. Sentence uses Ancient tier ability rolls and is the only source of Opt Atk Amp — another empty multiplier category.

Tier What to Roll
Special 3× Opt Atk Amp or 2x Opt Atk amp + 1x Opt Atk Increase → Whichever works best based on the math
Legend 3× Opt Atk Amp + 1× Opt Atk Increase
Mythic 3× Opt Atk Amp + 2× Opt Atk Increase
⚠ This is the first reasno why you stop at Special/Legend everywhere else. If you push non-sentence sources to Mythic you'll burn through dice and won't have enough for Sentence rolls — unless you get very lucky.
Star and Moon Blessing

This is a new abilities that just added in the latest update, and the only worth thing in this the Star and Moon option atk power on Ancient tier rolls and is the only source of Star and Moon option atk power — another empty multiplier category. HOWEVER I will only recommend to get 1 Star opt atk and 1 Moon opt attack.

Why only 1? Because this is the MOST EXPENSIVE ability roll in the entire game. Star and Moon Option Atk Power can only be earned through this system. However each Star ability roll cost 100.000 per roll and Moon cost 500.000 per roll. This is why I now make the legend opt atk power on number 4-6 becomes optional, because how scarce dice currency in this game and how much we need in the current state of the game. And getting the second star/moon atk will only give you 2x mult and become more and more diminishing instead of the original 5x for star and 10x for moon (back to math 101).
TLDR
  1. Always roll Option Atk Power — exclusive to this system, starts at zero
  2. Get everything to Special first
  3. Go for Legend if you want but I won't recommend it. At this rate Special is enough.
  4. At stage 45-1: Sentence is your priority — Opt Atk Amp exists nowhere else
  5. Grab only 1 Star Atk Amp, because the cost is hella expensive.
  6. Grab only 1 Mooon Atk Amp, because the cost is hella expensive (5x more expensive than the star roll).
