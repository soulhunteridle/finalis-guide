Ancient Rune Guide

Soul Hunter Idle AFK RPG · Individual value · High level perspective
Ranking criteria: Each rune is ranked based on
  1. The boost given expressed as a multiplier and
  2. its versatility across jobs and content.
  3. A universal new multiplier category outranks a higher raw number that is job locked or diminishing.
  4. Runes that form sets are ranked twice — once for solo value and once as a set.
  5. Number-dependent runes are split by milestone.
All multipliers assume 0 base for the stat being added (×N = 1 + stat%). Legend and Mythic rune combinations are covered in the 100 Days Guide.
