How Damage is Calculated
1
Calculate growth: Growth × Level
2
Add base damage: Base + (Growth × Level)
3
Total hits: Hit + Hit Count Increase
4
Multiply: Step 2 × Step 3
5
Apply multipliers using (1+x) formula: × (1 + Status Dmg%) × (1 + Increase Dmg%) × (1 + 10★ Bonus%) × (1 + 2nd Job Boost%)
6
Result = Final Damage
