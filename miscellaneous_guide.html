Titles

Always equip the title with the highest Title Atk Amp. Title Atk Amp is exclusive to titles — it exists nowhere else, so even a small amount is a new multiplier category.

Math reminder: Going from 0% to any % on an exclusive stat is always the biggest possible gain. Pick the highest number you have.

Summon Levels

Each summon type (equipment, skill, passive, hero, spirit) has 10 levels. Higher level = better rates and rarity. Every player reaches level 10 naturally over time.

If you followed the beginner guide, your equipment and passive summons are likely already level 10. For the rest:

  • Archmage build → push Skill summon to level 10 first
  • Dark Lord build → push Hero summon to level 10 first
Why level 10 and not 8? Level 8 unlocks the pickup banner, but level 10 gives the highest pull rate on it. Always go to 10 before pulling on pickup.
About Spirit Summon This summon is different than other summon because it didn't cost diamond to pull instead it requires its own specific currency from the spirit dungeon.

Dungeons
Growth · Enhancement · Abyssal Flame · Attribute · Skill Growth · Hero Growth
Use keys when you need to, but don't dump them all at once. Especially below stage 80 — growth is exponential there, so each key goes further if you're patient. Additional notes on growth dungeon, once you reach level 15000 your exp growth will start to diminish and after you reach level 20000 it will get diminished pretty hard. So after reaching level 20000 this specific dungeon key don't really do much for EXP and only do for gold.
Training Cave
Use this to test your DPS when changing runes or builds. Run a few times to confirm your damage is stable before committing.
Trial Tower + Treasure Dungeon
Use keys when you can secure at least one job level increase. Don't waste them on marginal progress.
2nd Job Advancement Trial Tower
Use keys when you can secure at least one 2nd job level increase. Don't waste them on marginal progress.
Spirit Dungeon
The only source for spirit summon and spirit level upgrade. Personally I'll hoard till I have enough to reach summon job level 8 and then use it again when the banner match with the spirit I use.
Rune Dungeon
Don't use keys until stage 16-1. Then ask on Discord or in-game for a carry to stage 60. Use your keys there to get Legend/Mythic runes matching your build. At stage 80-1, ask for another carry past stage 60.
World Boss
This is the only source to get abyssal essence, theres not much thing to do, just get your highest score here. And get the essence from weekly reward.
Ancient Ruins
Same logic as Trial Tower — use keys when you can upgrade at least 1 Ancient Symbol level from the run.

Use the currency to grab the key in this priority order:

  1. Treasure Dungeon ticket / 2nd Job Advancement Ticket (hoard these first good for progression)
  2. Trial Tower ticket (if Treasure sold out, for job level progression)
  3. Spirit Dungeon Ticket (if Trial Tower sold out, to help gain spirit summon level 8)

The order is NOT Absolute! The order I write on top is only the order for player that hasn't reach job 60. But once you reach job 60 each player might have their own preferences and priority. Equipment Amplification

Growth is linear with big jumps (x2 at 10 and 20, and x5 at 30 and 40) every 10 levels — cost also increases every 10 levels. With the new discovery of how ignore defense works and how I realize that scythe has 2 stats that matters, I will recommend to upgrade it in the order below:

  1. Scythe and Deco
  2. Orb
  3. Sentence

Why that order? Because of these reasons below
  1. Scythe have 2 stats that matters: Base atk and Increased base atk, believe it or not those 2 stats only comes from scythe (or like 99% of it)
  2. Deco have 2 stats that matters: Ignore def and Ignore def dmg, how these stats works? Check here
  3. Orb only have 1 stats that matters: Ele atk dmg, HP is not important.
  4. Sentence only have 1 stats: super crit dmg, and it's a chance based. so it's slightly lower than orb cause it's not guaranteed to hit.
Amplification works on the weapon stats hence why adding 20%strong> to orb will only get you from 100% to 120%, while deco and scythe will get you from 100% to 1.2x1.2 = 144%.
TLDR
  1. Upgrade Scythe and Deco first
  2. Upgrade Orb after
  3. Upgrade Sentence last
  4. Repeat every 10 level to hit that milestone boost

Abyss Equipment
Enhancement

Balance all pieces to 15 first → then all to 18 → then push to 20 in this order:

1
Emblem
Gives Super Crit Amp + Crit Amp — exclusive to emblem, starts at 0
2
Upper / Bottom / Shoes
Gives Crit Damage — currently scarce, small multiplier = high gain
3
Ring
Gives All Dmg Increase — not scarce, already stacked elsewhere
4
Necklace
Gives Super Crit Damage — inflated by a new feature, now lowest priority

You can also jump straight from 15 to 20 without balancing on 18 if you want and you feelin really lucky. Though I personally still prefer balancing on 18 first.

Same math principle: Emblem first because its stats are exclusive and start at zero — biggest multiplier jump possible.
Awakening
  • Emblem — focus on top 2 stats. Use (1+top)×(1+bottom)−1 to verify if a new roll is better than current
  • Everything else — focus on top stat only

For Emblem: if you already understand the formula, you can usually tell in a glance whether a new roll beats your current one without calculating.

TLDR
  1. Balance all to 15 → all to 18 → push to 20: Emblem first, then Upper/Bottom/Shoes, Ring, Necklace last
  2. Emblem is priority because its stats are exclusive and start at zero
  3. Awakening: Emblem → top 2 stats. Others → top stat only
  4. Use the formula to verify Emblem awakening rolls if unsure
